From Competition to Combat and Clinic

AID3D is a self-funded, purpose-built system for one mission: predicting which life-saving interventions a trauma patient will need before the critical window closes. We leverage advanced AI and machine learning to extract deep insights from data trauma teams already capture — no special sensors, no new workflows. Our system ingests existing clinical data natively and delivers results in real time, aligned to the cadences clinicians already follow. Built from the ground up to complement — not replace — existing TCCC and trauma bay protocols.
